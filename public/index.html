How to use Collexion

Collextion was designed by collectors for collectors and is meant to be as user-friendly as possible. Starting is as easy as making a profile and uploading photos of your collection.

About Us

We are a team of developers and hobbyists with interests ranging from sports to fashion to technology. We created Collextion after realized the need for a space where collections could not only sell and trade but primarily to showcase. Of course there are other options out there but we wanted to make a website customized to the needs of collectors.
